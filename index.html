About Us


We are a group of students from Queen's University Belfast exploring how technology can be used to achieve the UN Sustainable Development Goals. We operate within the school of Electronics, Electrical Engineering and Computer Science with support from a team of amazing staff members. We would love to partner with companies who use technology for societal good. Keep an eye out for our upcoming events and please get in contact with us if you / your company would be interested in getting involved!
